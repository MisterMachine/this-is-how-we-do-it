Our case study

  • Small website for a company called Made Tonight, Inc.
  • They have a proven business
  • Ready to take their web presence up a notch
  • Modest, but realistic budget
  • Requirements for new site
    • Design for a multi-device audience
    • Needs clear calls to active for their primary user flow
    • Evolve their established brand and style guide
  • Their sales are good
  • Their mobile traffic is rising, but dropoff is big
  • They have a modest, but decent budget and they understand the value of good design.
